1) Click on the start recording macro button.

2) Select all with ctrl+a.

3) Click on 'search' then click on 'replace'

4) Enter this in the first line:(\r?\n)|(\r\n?)

5) Enter this in the second line:\n (enter in \r\n here if you want to convert to \r\n format)

6) Make sure Regular Expression is checked in the 'Search Mode' and also select 'In Selection'

7) Click Replace all.

8) Stop recording the macro.

9) Playback the macro to see if it performs the operation.

10) Save the macro by giving it a name and optionally a shortcut.

11) Name it Replace EOL as LF

12) Also, make one for Replace EOL as CR+LF.
